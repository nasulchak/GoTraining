Logical Operators:
    ! (Logical NOT)
    || (Logical OR)
    && (Logincal AND)

Bitwise Operators:
    & (bitwise AND)
    | (bitwise OR)
    ^ (bitwise XOR)
    &^ (bit clear, AND NOT)
    << (left shift)
    >> (right shift)

Comparision Operators:
    == (equal)
    != (not equal)
    < (less than)
    <= (less than or equal to)
    > (greater than)
    >= (greater than or equal to)
